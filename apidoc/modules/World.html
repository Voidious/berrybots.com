Tables

Constants

Constants that help define the game rules and physics.

Fields:

  • SHIP_RADIUS: The radius of a ship.
  • LASER_SPEED: The speed at which a laser travels each tick.
  • LASER_HEAT: The number of ticks it takes for a laser gun to cool down before it can fire again.
  • LASER_DAMAGE: The amount of energy depleted by a laser when it hits.
  • TORPEDO_SPEED: The speed at which a torpedo travels each tick.
  • TORPEDO_HEAT: The number of ticks it takes for a torpedo gun to cool down before it can fire again.
  • TORPEDO_BLAST_RADIUS: The radius of the blast caused by a torpedo explosion. Any ship closer to the blast center will take damage.
  • TORPEDO_BLAST_FORCE: The magnitude of force from a torpedo blast to a ship located exactly at the center of a blast.
  • TORPEDO_BLAST_DAMAGE: The damage caused by a torpedo blast to a ship located exactly at the center of a blast.
  • DEFAULT_ENERGY: The amount of energy a ship starts with each game and each round.
  • MAX_THRUSTER_FORCE: The maximum magnitude of force a thruster can emit in one tick.
  • WALL_BOUNCE: The portion of the colliding force that is reflected back into a ship when it collides with a wall. A value of 1.0 would mean a ship loses no momentum when hitting a wall.
Wall

Specifies the position and size of a wall.

Fields:

  • left: The x coordinate of the left side of the wall.
  • bottom: The y coordinate of the bottom side of the wall.
  • width: The width of the wall.
  • height: The height of the wall.
Zone

Specifies the position, size, and tag of a zone.

Fields:

  • left: The x coordinate of the left side of the zone.
  • bottom: The y coordinate of the bottom side of the zone.
  • width: The width of the zone.
  • height: The height of the zone.
  • tag: The tag that identifies the zone. Not necessarily unique, and may be empty.
