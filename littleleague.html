BerryBots Little League

A series of casual tournaments to make BerryBots coding a bit more fun. Each tourney will use a different stage or rule set. Also, prizes.

First up: duels!

  • Format: 1-on-1 on Battle1, best of 7 matches
  • Deadline: February 26, 2014, 23:59 UTC
  • Prize: Choice of BerryBots t-shirt or $25 Amazon gift card
  • Procedure:
    • Round-robin (everyone faces everyone)
    • Overall win/loss determines seeding
    • Single-elimination tournament bracket
  • Full results and replays will be posted on February 27.

To enter, upload your bot to dbinbox/voidious or submit it through the BerryBots web UI. Please include an email address so I can authenticate you if you win. Submit as many bots as you like.

To join the discussion, check out the tourney thread in the forums.
